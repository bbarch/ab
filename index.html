About

Architecture is not an object.
It is a relationship.

I am an architect, landscape architect and systems thinker based in New Delhi, where I lead design at Babbar & Babbar Architects. For over three decades I have worked across India and the Middle East, from diplomatic missions and institutional campuses to housing, conservation and large-scale landscape planning.

My work explores the relationships between architecture, technology, cities and information—how drawings become buildings, landscapes become infrastructure, and better questions lead to more considered places.
